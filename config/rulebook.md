# Roster Rulebook

Version: 0.1

---

# 1. Centres

## RH

- Daytime centre
- Supplies RH SB1 and RH SB2 on monday, thursday, sunday for PT overnight team
- RH DM, RH CS1, RH SB1, RH SB2, RH AE performs daytime duties at RH

## PT

- 24-hour centre
- PT DM, PT CS1, PT CS2, PT CS/B, PT SB1, PT AE performs overnight duties at PT

---

# 2. Appointment Ownership

PT appointments

- PT DM
- PT CS1
- PT CS2
- PT CS/B
- PT SB1
- PT SB2
- PT AE

Only PT personnel may perform these.

RH appointments

- RH DM
- RH CS1
- RH SB1
- RH SB2
- RH AE

Only RH personnel may perform these.

---

# 3. Deployment

Monday, Thursday, Sunday

- RH sends RH SB1 and SB2
- PT CS/B is deployed

Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday

- RH sends RH SB1
- PT CS/B is not deployed

---

# 4. Point System

Weekday: 1

Friday: 1.5

Weekend: 2

Day Duty: 0.5

Public Holiday: Points same weightage as the respective day

---

# 5. Pre-plotted dates

- Reference workbook name: Leave_Off_Impt Dates Forecast
- Personnel who have plotted AL/OL/MA are not to be plotted for duty
- Personnel who have plotted HL/MC/WISDOM/SURGERY are not to be plotted for duty and until the end of their HL/MC
- Personnel who have plotted Outpro/ORD are not to be plotted for duty after their respective dates

---

# 6. Special Rules

All personnel must have a valid AMPT status in order to be plotted for duties

Reserve role

- Atleast one from each centre every day
- Plotted in the event someone in the team is unable to perform his duty
- No duty points awarded unless activated

DM Eligibility

- Must have completed 3x DM shadows
- Batch or rank seniority does not denote DM eligibility

DM Shadows

- Used only for training.
- Do not count as required staffing.
- Gets points as well

There must be a day break between overnight duties. non-negotiable

Placeholder Rows

- Rows 48–55 are placeholders.
- Ignore them.

Leaving Personnel

- Receive progressively fewer duties before departure.
- 3 months prior to leaving

PT SB2

- Exists every day except Saturday and Sunday
- Monday, Thursday and Sunday - Remains until daytime release. (applicable only to monday and thursday as sunday has no PT SB2)
